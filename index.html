Hear the choir from your singing position

Open this prototype in Safari on your iPhone. Select your voice part, sing along and compare three different choir spacings.

Before you begin

Prepare your AirPods Pro

On your iPhone, open Control Center, then press and hold the volume slider.

  1. Set Noise Control to Transparency.
  2. Turn off Conversation Awareness and Spatialize Stereo.

Use both AirPods Pro, find a quiet place and keep the same device volume throughout the comparison.

Step 1

Select your voice part

Choose the part you normally sing.

Step 2

Set your listening level

Play the sample and sing along briefly. Adjust the device volume until you can hear both yourself and the choir comfortably. Open the score while singing; it will follow the audio automatically.

Select your voice part first.

Step 3

Compare the three choir spacings

Sing at a comfortable and similar level in all three conditions. Do not change the device volume. The score follows the current playback position when you switch between spacings.
